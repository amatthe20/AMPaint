AMPaint Version 1.5.0  (Final) <br>

New Features: <br>
Mouse Coordinates - When the mouse is on the canvas, there are coordinates of it in the bottom left corner. <br>
If the mouse isn't on the canvas, there will be no coordinates (No Canvas Detected). <br>
Zoom In - Canvas gets zoomed in. <br>
Zoom Out - Canvas gets zoomed out. <br>

https://youtu.be/SV4_U8LhXI8 (Demo) <br>
